Jesture

Control your computer with your hands, not your keyboard.

Download for Linux

Jesture uses computer vision to read your hands, and will perform keyboard or mouse events for you when it sees a gesture.

Think pressing escape when you curl your hand into a fist, or moving the mouse to follow your hand when you point upwards.

Using the app

Pretty simple. Open the app up, go to the gesture list on the side.

Change the gestures here to your desired keybindings. Or, record a new gesture.

Perform your gesture in front of the camera! Press spacebar or click the button to stop recording.

Confirm your gesture, and you will now see the gesture in the list. Map the controls to whatever you like.

Just a note: the gesture recognition isn't 100% accurate. If you try a gesture once and it nothing happens, try again!
